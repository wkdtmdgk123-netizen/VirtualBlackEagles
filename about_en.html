Overview

The Black Eagles is the Republic of Korea Air Force's premier aerobatic team,
consisting of eight T-50B supersonic aircraft, demonstrating advanced flight
capabilities through various special maneuvers with exceptional teamwork.

Mission

The Black Eagles' mission is to inspire national pride by showcasing the Republic of Korea's air power and military capabilities. Through participation in international air shows and diplomatic activities, we contribute to enhancing Korea's national prestige, promoting defense exports, and advancing national interests. We also aim to recruit talented individuals to further the understanding and development of the Air Force.
